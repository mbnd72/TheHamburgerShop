Hamburger Trivia

When did Hamburger first appear in Germany?

The Hamburger, as we know it today, has a fascinating history with various claims to its origins. However, one widely accepted story is that it originated in the city of Hamburg, Germany, during the 19th century. At that time, a minced beef dish called "Hamburg steak" was popular. These early versions of hamburgers were typically made from minced or chopped beef. The concept of combining two slices of bread with a ground beef patty to create a "hamburger sandwich" likely emerged either in the United States or Germany. Before this, similar foods existed in European culinary traditions. (Source: Magazine )
